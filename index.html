Built For You, Differently

Our Approach

  • Manual matching in beta for quality.
  • Barter + Paid = Flexible.
  • City-by-city launch for local trust.
  • Always free for peer swapping.
  • Simple, mobile-friendly design.

Why It Works

  • Solves the teacher-learner gap with mutual swaps.
  • Focus on trust & safety with real profiles.
  • Early users get priority matching.
  • Retains users via community & real value.
  • No ads, no spam, just growth together.

Our Vision

“To build India’s most inclusive learning community where growth has no cost.”

Our Mission

“To empower anyone, anywhere to teach or learn a skill through exchange, not payment.”

Why Choose SwapEra?

Community-Powered Growth

We're not a company of teachers; we are a community of learners. Everyone is both a student and a mentor, creating a supportive, non-judgmental environment.

Trust & Safety First

With profile verification and community-based ratings, you can connect with trusted individuals from your own city, ensuring a safe and reliable learning experience.

Beyond Monetary Value

SwapEra operates on the principle that knowledge is priceless. We empower you to leverage your skills as a currency, making learning accessible to all, regardless of financial status.
