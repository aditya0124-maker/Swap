What Makes SwapEra Different

Our Approach
  • Manual matching in beta for better quality connections

  • Simple, mobile-friendly design
  • Real people, not bots
Why It Works
  • Solves the teacher-learner gap with mutual swaps

  • Focus on trust & safety with real profiles
  • Early users get priority matching
  • Retains users via community and real value
  • No ads, no spam, just growth together
