Why You'll Love SwapEra

🔁 Barter, Not Pay Your knowledge is your currency. Teach a skill to earn credits and learn a new one for free.
📍 Hyperlocal Connections Find and connect with fellow learners and teachers in your own city for trusted, face-to-face (or online) swaps.

What Makes SwapEra Different

Our Unique Approach
  • Barter-First Model: Your skills are valuable. Use them to learn, not just to earn.
  • Trust & Safety: We start with manual matching in beta to ensure quality connections.
  • Free & Unlimited Chat: No pay-per-message. Once matched, communication is open and free.
  • Community Focused: Earn more swaps by referring friends. The platform grows with you.
  • Simple & Transparent: No complex algorithms. See profiles, request swaps, and learn.
Key Features You'll Get
  • Skill Marketplace: A dashboard to find people who want to learn what you teach.
  • 5 Free Swaps: Every new user gets 5 free swaps to kickstart their journey.
  • Swap History: Keep track of your completed sessions and feedback.
  • Skill Swap Profiles: See what others offer and what they're looking for at a glance.
  • Direct Scheduling: Coordinate directly with your match to find the perfect time to learn.
